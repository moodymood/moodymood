User-defined gestures for Agumented Reality with Smart Phones.

Augmented Reality is a promising technology which opens a universe of new possibilities. However, when introducing new technologies, the first step should be understanding the effect on the user interaction.

Therefore I performed a guessability study to elicit end-user gestures for selection, zoom and rotation tasks with mobile phones on 15 partic ipants. Findings from the experiment allowed to define a gestures taxonomy for AR reality environments.
